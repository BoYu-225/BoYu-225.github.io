## Research Interests

Currently, I am primarily interested in machine learning theory, optimization and generative AI. Specifically, I am interested in two aspects of research:

1. **Practical theory**: I believe theory researchers should not be limited to derive some formula on the paper. Only in practice will the theory have an impact, as such I appreciate the works proposing new theories to solve long-existing real problems(for example, Muon and MuP). I prefer research on scientific discoveries to that on engineering (though the ability to make an idea work is also precious in the "scaling era"). Besides, my love on mathematics leads me to explore the unknown and uncover the latent structure in a more "mathematical" manner.

2. **Generative AI**: Attracted by the elegant mathematics structure, I am intersted in diffusion model, flow matching model, rectified flow model and so on. I believe many problems to solve could be modeled as classic math problems, and we should "stand on the shoulders of giants". The world is the same, but we can see it from a different perspective. The works I appreciate include *Score-Based Generative Modeling through Stochastic Differential Equations* by Yang Song, *DPM-Solver: A Fast ODE Solver for Diffusion Probabilistic Model Sampling in Around 10 Steps* by Cheng Lu and so on.
